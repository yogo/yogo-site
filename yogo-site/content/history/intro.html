The Historical NeuroSys System


Explore the original incarnation of NeuroSys-- take a tour through the system using the links to the left. While this is a historic version of NeuroSys, much of the same functionality will carry over to Yogo.

This tour will take you step-by-step through the processes of creating forms to store data, querying the database, modifying forms, and more. If you want to get a better idea of the capabilities and features of NeuroSys, this is a great place to start!
