Crux

Crux is a collaboration with Gully Burns at the University of Southern California. The goal of Crux is to test integration of 3rd party componants into the Yogo system. Dr. Burns is developing a modeling application using FLEX that specifically allows for the creation of KE-f-ED models in the Yogo system.
